What happens if I permanently delete my UnORG account?

Your profile, photos, information will be deleted. For deletion of orders data reach out to support@unorg.in.

Can you Deactivate your Account?

There is no such feature as Deactivating your account once you have deleted your account it will be deleted.

How to permanently delete your UnORG account?
  • Login to UnORG App
  • Enter profile section through Navbar available on bottom right corner of the app.
  • Delete account and you shall be informed that your account is deleted.
Can I cancel my account deletion?

No there is no such feature as cancellation of account deletion. Reach out to support@unorg.in for reactivation of your account.

For any other information and help you can contact at mail support@unorg.in
